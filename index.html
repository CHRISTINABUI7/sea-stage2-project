Coffee Types

Brazil

  • Less sweetness with a nutty aroma
  • Low acidity
  • Around 40 percent of the world's coffee production

Colombian

  • Notes of caramel and chocolate
  • Low acidity
  • Has antioxidant properties to protect the body against diseases

Vietnamese

  • Intense flavor with higher caffeine content
  • High acidity
  • High levels of antioxidants that lower blood pressure and body fat
